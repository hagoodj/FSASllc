About Us


FSAS is an online company specializing in the recovery and discounted redistribution of all types of salvaged freight. Our staff accurately identifies, photographs, inventories, and packages, and ships thousands of unique items weekly. Through this salvage operation, we are able to offer new and used merchandise, primarily through eBay, at significant discounts.

FSAS is guided by customer satisfaction, as well as irresistable deals. We are building connections with our customers through product experiences that are fast, mobile, and secure. You can find luxurious, simple, common, and rare items within our eBay store. With over 65,000 live listings and counting, our mission is to design a platorm where people can connect and confidently purchase items, many of which are unavailable anywhere else.
